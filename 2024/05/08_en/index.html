Your Enemy's Enemy Really Is Your Friend – According To Physics! - IFLScience

Humans navigate complex and evolving social networks. Social balance theory, originating in the 1940s, posits that individuals strive for equilibrium in relationships. It outlines rules like "friend of a friend is a friend" and "enemy of an enemy is a friend." Recent research, aligning with this theory, reveals the importance of constraints and individual positivity levels in modeling social networks accurately. The study, in Science Advances, sheds light on the mathematical underpinnings of social dynamics.

New Evidence for Our Solar System's Ghost: Planet Nine - Universe Today

Researchers Mike Brown and Konstantin Batygin, along with Alessandro Morbidelli and David Nesvorny, present more evidence supporting the existence of Planet Nine (P9). Their paper, "Generation of Low-Inclination, Neptune-Crossing TNOs by Planet Nine," published in The Astrophysical Journal Letters, discusses the anomalous dynamical structure in the solar system's distant reaches hinting at P9's presence. The study examines Trans-Neptunian Objects (TNOs) with conventional orbits through N-body simulations, focusing on 17 well-quantified orbits from 29 objects in the Minor Planet Database. The researchers aim to use these objects as a probe for P9, conducting simulations with and without P9 in the Solar System.
